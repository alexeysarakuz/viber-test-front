Our secret sauce

Our secret sauce

Your consumers are constantly talking about what they cook, order, and eat. They post about it on social media, they engage with recipes online, and they look at restaurant and delivery menus. The Tastewise Food Intelligence AI engine collects all of these data points in real time.

Our secret sauce

Then the data goes through processing and cleaning, separating the interesting stuff from the noise. The collected data is cross referenced with our massive database of ingredients, restaurants, locations, and historical consumer behavior to uncover insights - what are today’s hottest trends, and how do we predict and create tomorrow’s trends?

Our secret sauce

Finally, the information is distilled into easy-to-understand dashboards and reports that save you time by giving you the insights you need, quickly. Trend insights are valuable and can make a massive impact, but understanding why consumers choose to follow certain trends is what fuels sustainable growth. With the Tastewise, you get the full picture.
